Biography

I am a software engineer and system administrator enabling scientific computing. My day to day work supports engineering and scientific codes on high performance computing infrastructure. Occasionally I use cloud computing at home to test out ideas and learn new things.

This website is a personal endeavor, aimed at me thinking through concepts in my field of work and personal interests. You will probably find posts on technical topics only, and then some reflecting on faith and science/engineering.

In my spare time, I volunteer my time as the secretary and webmaster for the IEEE CT Section. I maintain an amateur radio open-source software project which provides Radio-over-IP (ROIP) services.
